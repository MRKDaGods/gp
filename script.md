# Graduation Expo Video Script (2-3 Minutes)

Target length: 2 minutes 30 seconds  
Style: Confident, clear, non-technical, impact-first  
Format: Mostly PowerPoint slides, light motion/zoom transitions

---

## Slide 1 (0:00-0:12)

Visual idea:  
Big title: "Seeing the City as One System"  
Subtitle: "Multi-Camera Tracking for Safer, Smarter Urban Mobility"  
Background: blurred city traffic + subtle camera frame overlays.

Voiceover:  
"Every day, cities generate massive amounts of video from different cameras. But each camera sees only a small piece of the story. Our  project Athar connects those pieces into one city-wide view."

---

## Slide 2 (0:12-0:30)

Visual idea:  
Problem slide with 3 short points appearing one-by-one:  
"Fragmented views"  
"Hard to follow movement across locations"  
"Slow, manual investigation"

Voiceover:  
"Today, camera systems are often isolated. Following a person or vehicle across multiple locations can be slow and difficult. That limits how quickly teams can react, investigate, and make informed decisions."

## Slide 3 (0:30-0:50)

Voiceover:

---

## On-Screen Style Notes

1. Keep text minimal: one message per slide.
2. Use large headlines and strong visuals, not paragraphs.
3. Use smooth fade/zoom transitions only; avoid flashy effects.
4. Keep color scheme consistent: dark background + 1 accent color.
5. Show impact words repeatedly: "faster," "clearer," "city-wide," "real-world."

## Delivery Notes

1. Speak at medium pace, around 125 to 140 words per minute.
2. Pause briefly after key lines like "one unified timeline."
3. Smile in voice on opening and closing for confidence.
4. If needed, trim to 2:00 by shortening slides 4 and 7.
